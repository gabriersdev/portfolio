## Overview

The main objective of the website is to serve as the digital business card for Agiliza Financiamento. It was designed to:

-   **Showcase the Company:** Communicate our 20 years of history, values, and mission.
-   **Detail Services:** Offer clear information about our specialties, such as MCMV, SBPE, and the use of FGTS.
-   **Educate the Client:** Through a blog with informative articles, positioning Agiliza as an authority in the sector.
-   **Generate Opportunities:** Capture contacts from potential clients through simulation and contact forms.

## Technologies Used

-   **CMS:** [Payload CMS](https://payloadcms.com/)
-   **Backend:** Node.js / Express.js
-   **Frontend:** Next.js / React
-   **Database:** SQLite
-   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
